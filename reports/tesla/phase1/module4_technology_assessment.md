## Executive Summary

Tesla maintains technology leadership in software integration and manufacturing innovation, but competitive advantages are narrowing rapidly. Key findings:

- **FSD Commercial Reality**: Still 3-5 years from Level 4 deployment despite progress
- **Manufacturing Innovation**: Unboxed Process provides most sustainable advantage

**Investment Implication**: Technology portfolio justifies premium through 2027-2028, then competitive convergence constrains technology-based returns.
