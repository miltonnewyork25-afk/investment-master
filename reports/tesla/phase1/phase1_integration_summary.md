1. **Business Model Transformation Success**: Tesla has successfully evolved beyond automotive, creating sustainable platform economics with $6B+ annual synergies

2. **Competitive Pressure Inflection**: 2025-2027 represents critical period where multiple competitive forces converge, particularly BYD global expansion

3. **Technology Advantage Compression**: Most valuable technology leads (FSD, software) compressing from 5+ years to 2-3 years due to competitive development

- **Maximum Allocation**: 8-12% (reduced from 15-20% pre-risk analysis)

- **Timeline**: 3-5 year investment horizon aligned with technology advantage window
- **Risk Budget**: Technology 40%, Market 30%, Regulatory 20%, Operational 10%

**Active Monitoring Requirements** (25 indicators):

- **Market Dynamics**: EV adoption rates, regulatory developments, consumer sentiment
- **Risk Indicators**: Safety incidents, supply chain disruptions, key person changes

**Accumulation Triggers** (Opportunity):
- FSD development setbacks creating temporary pessimism

- Manufacturing execution demonstrating sustainable cost advantages
- Energy business growth exceeding expectations

**Reduction Triggers** (Risk Management):

- BYD achieving cost parity in premium segments

- Chinese market share falling below 3% (competitive loss acceleration)

**Exit Triggers** (Strategic):
- Technology commoditization across multiple domains
- Competitive moats definitively breached by multiple players
- Regulatory environment turning decisively against autonomy
