## Module 1: Business Unit Mapping & Segmentation

### **1.1 Business Architecture Analysis** (Target Confidence: 9/10)
**Methodology**: Sum-of-the-Parts decomposition following Morgan Stanley approach

#### **Revenue Stream Identification**
- Automotive manufacturing (vehicles, regulatory credits)
- Software & services (FSD, Supercharging, insurance)
- Energy generation & storage (Solar, Powerwall, Megapack)
- Services & financing (maintenance, loans, leasing)

#### **Deliverables**:
- Complete business unit map with revenue attribution
- Profit margin analysis by segment
- Growth trajectory by business line
- Cross-business synergy identification

#### **Data Requirements**:
- **Tier 1**: Tesla 10-K segment reporting, cash flow statements
- **Tier 2**: Management commentary, investor presentations
- **Confidence Target**: 9/10 (based on audited financial data)

### **1.2 Operating Model Assessment** (Target Confidence: 8/10)
**Methodology**: Vertical integration analysis + operational efficiency benchmarking

#### **Key Focus Areas**:
- Manufacturing footprint and capacity utilization
- Supply chain integration vs. outsourcing decisions
- Technology development vs. licensing strategies
- Direct sales model vs. traditional dealer networks

#### **Deliverables**:
- Operating model diagram with cost structure
- Make-vs-buy decision analysis
- Operational efficiency benchmarks vs. peers
- Strategic risk assessment

## Module 2: Competitive Landscape Deep Dive

### **2.1 Multi-Dimensional Competitive Analysis** (Target Confidence: 7/10)
**Methodology**: Porter's Five Forces + BCG competitive analysis

#### **Competitive Frameworks**:

##### **Traditional Auto Competition** (BMW, Mercedes, Audi)
- Product positioning and pricing
- Manufacturing scale and cost structure
- Technology capabilities and R&D investment
- Brand strength and customer loyalty

##### **New EV Entrants** (Rivian, Lucid, NIO, BYD)
- Technology differentiation
- Manufacturing capability and scaling
- Capital efficiency and funding
- Market positioning and target segments

##### **Tech Giant Potential Entry** (Apple, Google, Amazon)
- Technology capabilities and advantages
- Capital resources and strategic intent
- Platform and ecosystem advantages
- Regulatory and manufacturing barriers

#### **Deliverables**:
- Comprehensive competitive matrix with scoring
- Competitive threat assessment by business unit
- Competitive advantage sustainability analysis
- Market share evolution projections

#### **Data Requirements**:
- **Tier 1**: Competitor financial filings, production data
- **Tier 2**: Industry reports, market share data
- **Tier 3**: Expert interviews, technology assessments
- **Confidence Target**: 7/10 (good data on current competitors, uncertainty on future entrants)

**Methodology**: Technical capability assessment + patent analysis

#### **Key Technology Areas**:

##### **Battery Technology**
- Energy density (Wh/kg) vs. competitors
- Cost per kWh vs. industry benchmarks
- Charging speed and cycle life
- Manufacturing scalability

##### **Autonomous Driving**
- Technical approach (vision vs. LIDAR)
- Miles driven and disengagement rates
- Safety performance vs. human drivers
- Regulatory approval status by region

##### **Manufacturing Innovation**
- Production efficiency metrics
- Quality scores and recall rates
- Automation level and labor productivity
- Time-to-market for new products

#### **Deliverables**:
- Technology capability matrix with confidence scores
- Patent landscape analysis
- Technology roadmap assessment
- Innovation pipeline evaluation
