## 第一章: Yield-Learning 模型层 - 良率的复利效应

### 1.1 理论框架：Wright's Law + Experience Curve

**核心公式**:

```
良率学习曲线 (Wright 1936):
Y(t) = Y₀ + (Y_max - Y₀) × (1 - e^(-k×t))

其中:
Y(t)   = t时刻的良率
Y₀     = 初始良率（tape-out时）
Y_max  = 理论最大良率
k      = 学习速率（学习能力的核心指标）
t      = 累计生产时间（月）

单位成本函数:
C(Y) = Wafer_Cost / (Chips_per_Wafer × Y)

推导:
∂C/∂Y = -Wafer_Cost / (Chips_per_Wafer × Y²) < 0
→ 良率每提升1%，成本下降 1/Y² (非线性)
```

**学术背书**:

1. Wright, T. P. (1936). "Factors affecting the cost of airplanes". *Journal of Aeronautical Sciences*
2. Arrow, K. J. (1962). "The Economic Implications of Learning by Doing". *Review of Economic Studies*
3. Gruber, H. (1992). "Learning by Doing in Semiconductor Industry". *Applied Economics*
4. Henderson, R. (1995). "Of Life Cycles Real and Imaginary: The Unexpectedly Long Old Age of Optical Lithography". *Research Policy*

## 第二章: EUV技术风险定价层 - 随机性的诅咒

### 2.1 EUV随机缺陷理论框架

**Stochastic Effects in EUV Lithography**

EUV光刻的随机缺陷来源于光子的量子特性，是先进节点的根本性挑战。

**核心物理**:

```
光子噪声 (Shot Noise):
σ_photon = √N / N = 1/√N

其中:
N = 单位面积光子数
σ = 相对噪声

推导:
- 13.5nm EUV光子能量: E = hc/λ = 92 eV
- vs 193nm DUV: E = 6.4 eV (低14x)
- 相同曝光能量下，EUV光子数仅为DUV的1/14
- 噪声σ = 1/√(N/14) = √14 × σ_DUV ≈ 3.7倍

结果:
→ EUV inherent噪声比DUV高3.7倍
→ 需要更多光子补偿 → 曝光时间↑ → 产能↓
→ 或接受噪声 → 随机缺陷↑ → 良率↓
```

**随机缺陷类型**:

1. **Stochastic Defects** (随机缺陷)
   - 微桥 (Micro-bridges): 应断未断
   - 针孔 (Pin-holes): 应连未连
   - 发生概率: ~10^-9 per feature
   - 但10nm芯片有10^11+ features → 每片100+缺陷

2. **LER/LWR** (线边/线宽粗糙度)
   - LER (Line Edge Roughness): ±1.5nm @7nm节点
   - LWR (Line Width Roughness): ±2.0nm
   - 影响: 电阻变化±15%, 性能±5%

3. **Mask Defects** (光罩缺陷)
   - EUV mask缺陷容忍度: <0.1 defects/cm²
   - vs DUV: <1 defect/cm²（严格10倍）

**学术背景**:

1. De Bisschop, P. (2018). "Stochastic effects in EUV lithography". *SPIE Advanced Lithography*
2. Gronheid, R. (2019). "Stochastic printing failures in EUV lithography". *Microelectronic Engineering*
3. IMEC (2021). "EUV Stochastic Roadmap"
